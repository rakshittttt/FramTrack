🛠️ Seasonal Maintenance Calendar

🌱 Spring (Mar-May)

  • Change engine oil and filters
  • Check tire pressure and tread
  • Inspect hydraulic fluid levels
  • Lubricate all grease points

☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug)

  • Clean air filters frequently
  • Monitor coolant levels
  • Check belt tension
  • Inspect radiator for clogs

🍂 Monsoon (Jul-Sep)

  • Waterproof electrical connections
  • Check brake fluid levels
  • Inspect for rust formation
  • Clean fuel tank water separator

❄️ Winter (Oct-Feb)

  • Use winter-grade oils
  • Check battery condition
  • Inspect fuel system
  • Store properly if not in use
